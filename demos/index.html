tiny transformers

Same 1-layer GPT architecture, different vocabularies. Each demo trains a ~7K parameter transformer to predict sequences, then generates continuations from your input.

Draw a seed waveform, train on quantized sine waves, watch the model continue your drawing. 16 amplitude levels.
vocab: 16 amplitudes · input: freehand draw

Tap notes on a piano roll, train on simple melodies, hear the model improvise. Web Audio playback.

Paint a few pixels, train on grayscale patterns, watch the model fill in the row. Tiny image generation.
vocab: 16 gray levels · input: pixel grid · coming soon

Tap a beat pattern, train on rhythms, let the model extend your groove. Drum machine UI with Web Audio.
